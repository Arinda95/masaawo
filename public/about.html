M A S A A W O

About us

Masaawo is a tool created by an individual to help prevent criminal attacks through the public tranportation system by providing user submitted information to help users identify and avoid criminal transport vehicles. After a recent rise in vehicular robberies, many of which affected people close to me i sought to use my skills to mobilize the public to keep our tranportation system safe and to provide tools for members of the general public to safeguard themselves.
